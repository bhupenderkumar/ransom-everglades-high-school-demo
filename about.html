Our Mission & History

With a rich history dating back to 1903, Ransom Everglades High School has been a cornerstone of Miami's educational landscape for over a century. Our school's founders were pioneers in education, recognizing the importance of academic rigor, character development, and outdoor exploration. Today, we continue to build on this legacy, offering a unique and enriching experience for our students.

At Ransom Everglades, we believe that teaching is not just about imparting knowledge, but also about inspiring curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Our expert faculty use innovative and engaging approaches to bring subjects to life, and our small class sizes ensure that every student receives personalized attention and support.

Beyond the classroom, Ransom Everglades offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, sports, and clubs that cater to diverse interests and talents. From debate and robotics to the arts and athletics, we encourage our students to explore their passions and develop their skills outside of the classroom.
