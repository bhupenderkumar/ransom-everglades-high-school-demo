Our Mission & History

Ransom Everglades High School has a rich history dating back to 1903, when it was founded as a humble elementary school. Over the years, we have grown and evolved into a thriving community of learners, committed to academic excellence and character development. Our school's strong sense of tradition and values is rooted in our commitment to the well-being and success of every student.

At Ransom Everglades, we believe that learning should be a collaborative and engaging experience. Our faculty uses a variety of teaching approaches to meet the diverse needs of our students, from project-based learning to hands-on experimentation. We encourage our students to take risks, explore new ideas, and develop a growth mindset that serves them well throughout their lives.

From the athletic fields to the arts studios, our students have countless opportunities to explore their passions and develop new skills. Whether they're competing in a varsity sport, performing in a school play, or creating art in our studios, our students are encouraged to try new things and pursue their interests with confidence and creativity.
