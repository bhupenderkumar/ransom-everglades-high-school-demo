Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed to provide a nurturing and engaging environment for children aged 3-5, fostering social, emotional, and cognitive development.

📚

Elementary School

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-5, offering a rigorous and hands-on curriculum that prepares students for success in the upper grades.

🌐

Middle and Upper School

Our Middle and Upper School program serves students in grades 6-12, offering a comprehensive and challenging curriculum that prepares students for college and beyond.

💻

STEM Program

Our STEM program offers a range of courses and activities that foster a love of science, technology, engineering, and math, providing students with a competitive edge in an increasingly technical world.

🎭

Arts Program

Our Arts program offers a range of courses and activities that foster creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts, providing students with a well-rounded and enriching educational experience.

🏈

Athletics Program

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports and activities that foster teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness, providing students with opportunities to develop new skills and compete at the highest levels.
