Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program offers a play-based learning experience for 2.5 to 5-year-old children, laying the foundation for a lifelong love of learning.

🎓

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 5th grade, focusing on core subjects, arts, and physical education.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School program serves students from 6th to 12th grade, offering a rigorous curriculum, electives, and extracurricular activities.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program provides students with hands-on learning experiences in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, preparing them for careers in these fields.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program offers courses and extracurricular activities in music, theater, dance, and visual arts, allowing students to express themselves creatively.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports teams for students, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
