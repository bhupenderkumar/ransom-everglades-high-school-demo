Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Early Childhood Program provides a nurturing and engaging environment for children as young as 2 years old, where they can begin their educational journey and develop essential social, emotional, and cognitive skills.

📚

Elementary School

In our Lower School, students in grades K-5 receive a well-rounded education that includes reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies, as well as music, art, and physical education.

📊

Middle School

Our Middle School program for grades 6-8 emphasizes academic rigor, creativity, and problem-solving skills, as students begin to explore their interests and develop their abilities in a supportive and challenging environment.

🤝

Upper School

In our Upper School, students in grades 9-12 are encouraged to take ownership of their learning, pursue their passions, and develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in college and beyond.

💻

STEM Program

Our STEM program provides students with a rigorous and hands-on education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, including coding, robotics, and engineering design.

🎭

Arts Program

Our Arts Program offers a range of courses and extracurricular activities in music, theater, dance, and visual arts, where students can express their creativity and develop their artistic talents.
